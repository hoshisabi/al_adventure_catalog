description

Have you finally reached the end of your journey, or has it just begun? And who is the little monster that you’ve just picked up?

Play it stand alone, or as part 3 of the “Little AL Adventures” series.

An irreverent story that pays homage to both classic children's books as well as horror movies.

A Two-Hour Adventure for Tier 1 characters, Optimized for APL 3.

Seed used: Unexpected Passenger
Content Warnings: possession, starvation (mention), murder, body horror, paranoia, madness

(this adventure is in the process of final edits, and uploaded to meet the deadline: be aware that there will still be changes over the next few days).

This adventure was set to debut at U-Con on November 2023 (http://www.ucon-gaming.org/) The mascot for the PandoDnD server, P4, finally makes an appearance in an adventure!

Please see other adventures written for U-Con.

Please see other adventures connected to the PandoDnD server.

If you see any errors or have any suggestions, or just wish to retell the tale of how your group went through the story, feel free to contact the author at:
